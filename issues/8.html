bug #8

The method 'jobCategories' should also be accessible outside of a jobSession

Added by Stefan Klauck over 10 years ago. Updated about 10 years ago.

Status:rejected Start date:05/14/2012
Priority:Normal Due date:
Assignee:Peter Tröger % Done:

0%
Category:-
Target version:-

Description

Job categories can be set in job AND reservation templates.

Given one wants to know the appropriate job category for a reservation and does not know the valid job categories. Conforming the current specification, one has to open (or create) a job session to be able to call jobCategories on it.

Proposals to solve the issue

1) move the jobCategories method to the SessionManager
2) [or] move the jobCategories method to the "light weight" MonitoringSession
3) [or] add the jobCategories method to the ReservationSession
