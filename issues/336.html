Per the standards council telecon of January 11, this is now approved for 60-day public comment. OGF is working on a email server migration, which we will complete before announcing public comment.

Once it is announced, the WG and community members should be asked to read & review the document.

Standards council noted that the header for section 14 says, "Intellectual Attributes Statement," but it is the standard OGF boilerplate "Intellectual Property Statement." While the document does have discussion of intellectual attributes, it is preferable that the section label follow the OGF standard of "Intellectual Property Statement."
