Can an unordered sequence be empty

Added by Steve Hanson almost 9 years ago

Spec says that a sequence can have empty content (as long as it is not the content of a complex type).
The implication is that this applies to both ordered and unordered sequences.
But an unordered sequence gets rewritten to a choice, which never allows empty content.
So is an empty unordered sequence allowed or not?
