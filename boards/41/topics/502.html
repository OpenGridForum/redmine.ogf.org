Correction of schema for NSI CS v2.1

Added by Guy Roberts about 4 years ago

There is an issue with the QueryType structure in the query* request messages. The ifModifiedSince attribute in the wrong location, restricting its use, and not corresponding to the document definition.

Here is the segment:

&lt;xsd:complexType name="QueryType"&gt;
        &lt;xsd:sequence&gt;
            &lt;xsd:choice&gt;
                &lt;xsd:element name="connectionId"        type="ftypes:ConnectionIdType" minOccurs="0"maxOccurs="unbounded" /&gt;
                &lt;xsd:element name="globalReservationId" type="tns:GlobalReservationIdType" minOccurs="0"maxOccurs="unbounded" /&gt;
                &lt;xsd:element name="ifModifiedSince"     type="xsd:dateTime" minOccurs="0" /&gt;
            &lt;/xsd:choice&gt;
        &lt;/xsd:sequence&gt;
    &lt;/xsd:complexType&gt;

Here is what it should look like:

&lt;xsd:complexType name="QueryType"&gt;
        &lt;xsd:sequence&gt;
            &lt;xsd:choice&gt;
                &lt;xsd:element name="connectionId"        type="ftypes:ConnectionIdType" minOccurs="0" maxOccurs="unbounded" /&gt;
                &lt;xsd:element name="globalReservationId" type="tns:GlobalReservationIdType" minOccurs="0" maxOccurs="unbounded" /&gt;
            &lt;/xsd:choice&gt;
            &lt;xsd:element name="ifModifiedSince"     type="xsd:dateTime" minOccurs="0" /&gt;
        &lt;/xsd:sequence&gt;
    &lt;/xsd:complexType&gt;

This should be updated before publication. Note: this will require an update to a diagram and the XSD at the end of the document.
