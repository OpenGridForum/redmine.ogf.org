Guidelines

Added by Redmine Admin over 9 years ago

The public comment area is intended to gather feedback on documents which are about to be published by the Open Grid Forum. For detailed information on the OGF Document Process, please read GFD.152 .

This forum is not intended to be a location for debate, although exchanges which aim to clarify submitted comments are more than welcome. Document authors are encouraged to submit separate discussion forums within their respective Redmine projects for extended discussion threads.

Any Redmine member can submit comments in this forum. For people who wish to remain anonymous, please send your comments to public_comments@ogf.org -- they will be added to the respective forum anonymously.
